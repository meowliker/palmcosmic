1. Refund Scope for One-Time Purchases

AstroRekha currently offers one-time digital purchases only. We do not operate recurring customer billing at this time.

✓ Scope Clarification

This policy applies to one-time payments made on astrorekha.com.

Since purchases are one-time, there is no cancellation flow or renewal cycle to manage.

For refund-related assistance, please email team.astrorekha@gmail.com with your transaction details.

2. What to Do to Request a Refund

Refunds are not automatic. We only process refunds for genuine, verifiable reasons. If you believe your case qualifies, you may submit a refund request that meets the following criteria:

📋 Refund Eligibility Criteria

  1. You made a purchase directly on our website astrorekha.com
  2. You contacted us via email within 14 days of the purchase date
  3. You provide sufficient evidence showing a genuine issue (for example: duplicate charge, technical delivery failure, or unauthorized payment)

Required Information for Refund Request

Please send us a written application to team.astrorekha@gmail.com containing the following information:

  • The email address related to your purchase
  • Payment method used (Card, PayPal, etc.)
  • The payment date
  • Payment receipt or any payment confirmation
  • A brief explanation of the reason for the refund request

⚠️ Please Note

Meeting the criteria above allows us to review your case. Approval is not guaranteed and is decided only after verification.

For clarity, this Money-Back Policy does not apply to:

  • Personal reasons (e.g., you dislike the product)
  • Financial reasons (e.g., you did not expect to be charged, or you changed your mind after successful purchase)
  • One-time purchases that were delivered successfully and used as intended

Refund Processing

We will review your request and notify you by email whether it is approved. If approved, your refund will be sent to your original payment method, typically within 7 to 30 business days depending on payment provider processing timelines.

3. Important Statement

⚠️ Non-Refundable Fees

Generally, if you do not meet the conditions of our Money-Back Policy set out above, the fees you have paid are non-refundable and non-exchangeable unless otherwise stated herein or as required by applicable law.

All refund decisions are made on a case-by-case basis and are granted only when we determine there is a genuine and verifiable issue.

However, if you terminate the agreement during the withdrawal period, you have the right to receive a refund proportionate to the provided service.
