1. Refund Scope

PalmCosmic offers subscription access, trial-based subscription offers, optional one-time report unlocks, and optional coin packages. This policy explains how refund requests are reviewed for those digital products.

✓ Scope Clarification

This policy applies to payments made through the PalmCosmic web application or its approved payment processor checkout flows.

Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled before the next billing date. Canceling a subscription stops future renewal charges but does not automatically refund charges already processed.

For refund-related assistance, please email weatpalmcosmic@gmail.com with your transaction details.

2. What to Do to Request a Refund

Refunds are not automatic. We only process refunds for genuine, verifiable reasons. If you believe your case qualifies, you may submit a refund request that meets the following criteria:

📋 Refund Eligibility Criteria

  1. You made a purchase, subscription payment, renewal payment, report unlock, or coin purchase through PalmCosmic or its approved checkout flow
  2. You contacted us via email within 14 days of the purchase, renewal, or charge date
  3. You provide sufficient evidence showing a genuine issue (for example: duplicate charge, technical delivery failure, or unauthorized payment)

Required Information for Refund Request

Please send us a written application to weatpalmcosmic@gmail.com containing the following information:

  • The email address related to your purchase
  • Payment method used, as shown by the payment processor
  • The payment, renewal, or subscription start date
  • Payment receipt, subscription ID, checkout confirmation, or transaction ID if available
  • A brief explanation of the reason for the refund request

⚠️ Please Note

Meeting the criteria above allows us to review your case. Approval is not guaranteed and is decided only after verification.

For clarity, this Money-Back Policy does not apply to:

  • Personal reasons (e.g., you dislike the product)
  • Financial reasons where the checkout or subscription renewal terms were disclosed before payment
  • Subscription charges after a trial or renewal where the user did not cancel before the stated billing date
  • One-time report unlocks, coin purchases, or digital content that were delivered successfully and used as intended

Refund Processing

We will review your request and notify you by email whether it is approved. If approved, your refund will be sent to your original payment method, typically within 7 to 30 business days depending on payment provider processing timelines.

3. Important Statement

⚠️ Non-Refundable Fees

Generally, if you do not meet the conditions of our Money-Back Policy set out above, the fees you have paid are non-refundable and non-exchangeable unless otherwise stated herein or as required by applicable law.

All refund decisions are made on a case-by-case basis and are granted only when we determine there is a genuine and verifiable issue.

The withdrawal right may not apply if the performance of the agreement has begun with your prior express consent and your acknowledgment that you thereby lose your right of withdrawal.

However, if you terminate the agreement during the withdrawal period, you have the right to receive a refund proportionate to the provided service.

BY STARTING A SUBSCRIPTION, ACTIVATING A TRIAL, OR MAKING A PURCHASE, YOU MAY EXPRESSLY CONSENT TO THE IMMEDIATE PERFORMANCE OF THE AGREEMENT AND ACKNOWLEDGE THAT YOU MAY LOSE YOUR RIGHT OF WITHDRAWAL ONCE ACCESS, DIGITAL CONTENT, COINS, OR THE APPLICABLE REPORT IS SUCCESSFULLY DELIVERED TO YOU.

Therefore, you will not be eligible for a refund unless the digital content or services are defective.

If you reside in California, Connecticut, or another jurisdiction with specific cancellation rights, we will honor refund or cancellation rights required by applicable law. Please contact us promptly with your registered email and transaction details.
